BotWatch — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

BotWatch is a browser extension that estimates whether a Twitter/X account is likely fake/bot or real using a machine-learning model. This policy explains what data the extension handles and why.

What data we process

When you view a Twitter/X profile, BotWatch reads only the publicly visible information shown on that profile page:

  • The account's screen name (handle)
  • Follower count and following count
  • Post (tweet) count
  • Whether the profile has a bio, a website link, and a location
  • The account's join date

BotWatch does not read your tweets, direct messages, your own account credentials, your browsing history, or the content of any other website.

How the data is used

The profile values above are sent to our prediction service at botwatch-6qpn.onrender.com solely to compute a real/fake verdict, which is returned to the extension and shown to you. The data is processed in memory to generate that prediction and is not stored in a database, profiled, or used for any other purpose.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use the data for advertising or tracking.
  • We do not create user profiles or require you to sign in.
  • We do not use cookies for tracking.

Permissions

The extension requests only the permissions it needs to function:

  • Access to twitter.com and x.com — to read the public profile statistics on pages you visit.
  • Access to our API host — to send those statistics for a prediction and receive the result.
  • activeTab / scripting / tabs — to read the profile in the tab you are viewing, run the extension's own script on that page, and update the toolbar icon as you navigate.

Data retention

We do not retain the profile data after a prediction is returned. Standard, transient server request logs may be generated by our hosting provider for operational and security purposes and are not used to identify or track individuals.

Children's privacy

BotWatch is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
